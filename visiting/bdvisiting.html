Location
If your vacation plan is to know more about the history of the place, then Baroda Museum & Picture Gallery will provide you the opportunity to have a glimpse into the past of Vadodara and its history. Designed by Mant and Chisholm and built in the year 1894 by Sayajirao, this museum houses many artifacts ranging from structures and carved ceramics to architectural pieces and an Egyptian mummy. Here, you will find antique pieces from China, Nepal and Japan, among other countries. There is also a famous gallery with various pictures, including those of Tibetan arts, most of which are from the private collection of Sayajirao. The museum has a Zoology section, where you will find the amazing blue-whale skeleton, which is 22m long. Apart from all these, there is a library which houses almost 23,000 books and journals and a section that focuses on the various ethinicities settled in Gujarat
